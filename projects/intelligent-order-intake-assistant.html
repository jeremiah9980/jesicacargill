AI Intake product concept

Intelligent Order Intake Assistant

A concept for using AI to read incoming contract and order details, structure file data, flag missing information, and prepare a clean opening workflow for title teams.

ProblemUsersWorkflowSample screensAI opportunities

Problem

Order intake is often manual, repetitive, and inconsistent. Missing data at file open creates downstream delays, rework, and communication gaps that title teams end up fixing later under time pressure.

  • Email attachments arrive in different formats
  • Teams retype the same data into multiple systems
  • Missing lender, HOA, payoff, or property details get discovered too late

Users

  • Escrow assistants opening files
  • Escrow officers reviewing intake quality
  • Title / curative teams receiving handoffs
  • Operations managers measuring intake quality and speed

Workflow

How the product concept works

1. Intake capture

AI reads incoming contracts, order sheets, email instructions, and attachments as they arrive.

2. Structured extraction

The assistant identifies buyer, seller, property, lender, contract dates, earnest money, and key transaction attributes.

3. Validation + handoff

It flags missing items, generates an intake summary, and prepares a clean opening checklist for the title team.

AI opportunities

  • Document extraction across contracts, addenda, and lender forms
  • Confidence scoring for each extracted field
  • Missing-information prediction based on transaction patterns
  • Suggested communication drafts for follow-up requests
  • Automatic creation of intake checklists and structured handoff notes

Business impact

This concept could reduce file-open delays, improve data quality earlier in the lifecycle, and give title teams a cleaner operational starting point. It fits naturally into a broader operating system focused on visibility, communication, and workflow standardization.
