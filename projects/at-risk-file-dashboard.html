Workflow Visibility

At-Risk File Dashboard

A stage-based dashboard concept for showing bottlenecks, missing items, stalled tasks, and deadline risks before they turn into customer problems.

Problem

By the time a file feels "on fire," the underlying warning signs have often been visible for days. The problem is that most teams do not have a simple way to see stalled files, deadline risk, or ownership gaps early enough.

Concept

A workflow dashboard that groups files by stage, highlights missing requirements, flags stalled activity, and surfaces deadline risk so teams can intervene before clients or referral partners feel the impact.

Signals to track

  • Missing title or curative items
  • No movement by stage over time
  • Closing date proximity
  • Unassigned next action

Operational value

  • More proactive management
  • Less deadline surprise
  • Stronger accountability by role

Why Jesica fits it

She knows where files tend to stall and which seemingly small misses end up creating closing pressure later.

Future direction

This concept turns operational intuition into visible action.

It helps teams work from the same reality instead of relying on scattered emails, memory, or last-minute triage.
